About Me

Hello! I'm Mackenzie Burt, a passionate 3D modeler, animator, and digital artist. I specialize in character design, concept art, and interactive experiences. My mission is to craft visually stunning and impactful projects that inspire and captivate audiences. What I strive towards most is allowing players and audiences in general to truly feel immersed. I want them to feel the pain of loss that the characters experience. The joys of successfully solving the equation that allows multi-dimensional travel. The numbness of the loss of hope during dark times. If they don't feel it, they won't relate to it, and they won't remember the light at the end, or the need for both the light and the dark.
